We thought now would be a good time to publish our campaign finances rather than wait until after the election. This is primarily to nip in the bud any concerns voters may have that a disproportionate amount of money has been spent on Scott's campaign that may be giving him an 'unfair' advantage.

Scott is fortunate enough to have tech-savvy volunteers, so a lot of the 'fancy' stuff you're seeing is not actually that hard to do if you have the skills.

Below we have set out how much has been spent in various aspects of Scott's campaign and where this money came from.

All amounts shown in the tables below are in GBP and are correct as at the date of publication.
